Inspiration of the song

"The inspiration for this song came from a conversation that the author had with her girlfriend. The author clarifies that she likes orange but her girlfriend likes blue. "I used colors to metaphor two of us. It is very interesting that orange and blue are two completely different feelings. What did they go through to finally get together?"AD02 said."
